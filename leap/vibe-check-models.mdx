# Vibe-check models

Before integrating a model into your application, it's essential to test its performance and behavior with your specific use cases. "Vibe-checking" allows you to evaluate how well a model responds to your prompts, assess response quality, and understand its capabilities and limitations.

LEAP provides multiple ways to interact with and test LFM2 models without any setup or configuration. Whether you prefer testing on your device or in the cloud, you can quickly validate that a model meets your requirements before moving to full deployment.
